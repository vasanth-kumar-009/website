Step into the world of Agile project management with Agile Scrum in Practice, a hands-on course designed to bridge the gap between theory and real-world implementation. This course goes beyond the basics to show how Agile and Scrum methodologies are applied in actual team settings. You'll explore key Scrum roles, ceremonies, and artifacts while engaging in practical simulations that mirror real project scenarios.

By the end of the course,I gain

  • A solid understanding of Agile values and Scrum principles
  • Practical experience in Sprint Planning, Daily Standups, Sprint Reviews, and Retrospectives
  • Insight into user stories, product backlogs, and velocity tracking
  • Skills to collaborate effectively in Agile teams and contribute as a Scrum Master, Product Owner, or Developer
